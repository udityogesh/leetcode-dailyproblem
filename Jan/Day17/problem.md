## Problem Summary

You are given multiple axis-aligned rectangles defined by their bottom-left and top-right coordinates.  
Your task is to determine the **maximum possible area of a square** that can be placed **inside the overlapping region of any two rectangles**.

If no two rectangles overlap, the result should be `0`.
